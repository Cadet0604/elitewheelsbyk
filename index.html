Our Story

BORN IN DELHI

The Story

BUILT FOR RIDERS
BY A RIDER

EliteWheelsByK was born out of frustration — the frustration of a Delhi car enthusiast who couldn't find a rental service that matched his standards. No shady condition cars. No hidden costs. No terrible customer service.

So Kunal Makkar decided to build it himself. Starting with just two vehicles and a phone number shared in WhatsApp groups, EliteWheelsByK grew into Delhi's freshest automotive rental experience — entirely through word-of-mouth, raw passion, and a simple belief: every ride should feel elite.

We're a Delhi-born startup that hustles hard for Gen Z and millennial riders who want premium experiences without the premium attitude. Whether it's a weekend escape to the hills, a birthday flex, a corporate event, or just a Tuesday that deserves a Mustang — we got you.

👑
KUNAL MAKKAR
Founder & CEO · EliteWheelsByK

Delhi-based entrepreneur, car fanatic, and firm believer that life's too short for boring rides. Kunal built EliteWheelsByK from the ground up with a mission to democratize access to premium vehicles — no gatekeeping, just good vibes and great machines.

Our Values

WHAT WE STAND FOR

  • 01

    Radical Transparency

    What you see is what you pay. No fine print, no surprise charges, no excuses. Every price is upfront, every condition is stated clearly.

  • 02

    Premium or Nothing

    Every vehicle in our fleet is thoroughly inspected, serviced, and cleaned before every rental. If it doesn't meet our standard, it doesn't roll out.

  • 03

    You're Not a Booking ID

    We remember our customers. Kunal personally monitors every booking. You'll always reach a real person who actually cares.

  • 04

    Safety is Non-Negotiable

    Every ride is fully insured. Our bikes come with helmets. Our cars carry first-aid kits. We take safety as seriously as performance.
